Arman Raayatsanati

I am an undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at ETH Zurich. My current research interests include the intersection of robotics, machine learning, computer vision, optimization, and computer architecture.

At ETH, I have worked on Raptor, a quadcopter equipped with a soft gripper designed for the rapid picking up of objects. I am currently working on my undergraduate thesis at the Soft Robotics Lab under the supervision of Robert Katzschmann.
